Frequently asked

Honest answers.

How is this different from a franchise?+

A franchise charges an upfront brand fee (₹15–50 lakh typically) plus ongoing royalty (5–15% of revenue) and binds you to strict operational rules. Our programme has zero brand fee, zero royalty — only a transparent revenue share on each order we send you. You keep your independence, your kitchen name, and can leave anytime with 30 days notice.

What's the revenue share?+

Annam takes 28–35% of each order's value, depending on cuisine complexity and city. The rest goes to you to cover ingredients, packaging, gas, electricity, and your earnings. We disclose the exact percentage during the discovery call. As you scale, the share trends to the lower end of that range — better margins for committed kitchens.

What if I don't have ₹2 lakh to invest?+

We help you apply for Mudra loans (up to ₹10 lakh, low interest) and Stand-Up India loans (specifically for SC/ST/women entrepreneurs, up to ₹1 crore). Most kitchen-partners use a mix of personal savings + Mudra. Our team walks you through the paperwork.

Why does management approval matter?+

One bad kitchen can ruin Annam's reputation for thousands of customers. Our quality team reviews every kitchen — hygiene, equipment, recipe execution, packaging — before activation. No kitchen receives orders until management approval is granted. If something doesn't meet standards, we tell you exactly what to fix and re-audit free of charge.

Can men apply?+

Absolutely yes — our programme is open to everyone. We highlight the women-entrepreneur angle because India's home cooks are predominantly women whose talent is structurally undervalued, and we want to fix that. But the application process and economics are identical regardless of gender.

What cities are you in?+

Currently active: Mumbai, Pune, Bangalore, Hyderabad. Launching in 2026: Chennai, Delhi NCR, Kolkata. If you're outside these cities, you can still apply — we'll add you to the waitlist and prioritise expansion based on demand.

How many hours a day do I need to work?+

Starter kitchens (30–60 meals/day): typically 4–5 hours, mostly during lunch and dinner prep windows. Pro kitchens (150+ meals/day): 7–9 hours with one or two helpers. You set your daily capacity in the vendor app — if you can only cook 30 meals on a given day, we won't send you 50.
